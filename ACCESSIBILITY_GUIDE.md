# ♿ CivicSense - Accessibility Guide

## 🎯 Making CivicSense Accessible for Everyone

CivicSense is now designed to be accessible for low-educated users, elderly citizens, and people with disabilities.

---

## 📱 Simple Mode Interface

### Location: `simple-report.html`

**Features for Low-Educated Users:**

### 1. 🎨 Visual Icon-Based Interface
- **Large Icons** (48px) for each problem type
- **Emoji Icons** that are universally understood:
  - 🕳️ Pothole
  - 🗑️ Garbage
  - 💡 Street Light
  - 💧 Water Leak
  - 🚰 Drainage
  - 🛣️ Road Damage
  - 🌳 Park Issue
  - 🚦 Traffic

### 2. 🎤 Voice Input (Speak Instead of Type)
- **Voice Description**: Speak the problem instead of typing
- **Voice Name Input**: Speak your name
- **No Reading Required**: Just speak naturally
- **Supported Languages**: English, Hindi (ready for more)

**How to Use:**
1. Click microphone button (🎤)
2. Speak clearly
3. System converts to text automatically

### 3. 📷 Photo-Based Reporting
- **Take Photo** button with camera icon
- **Large buttons** (easy to tap)
- **Instant preview** of photo
- **No typing needed** - photo shows the problem

### 4. 📍 One-Tap Location
- **Single button** to get location
- **Automatic detection** - no address needed
- **Visual confirmation** with checkmark
- **No manual entry required**

### 5. 🔢 Step-by-Step Process
**5 Simple Steps:**
1. **Choose Problem** (tap icon)
2. **Take Photo** (optional)
3. **Get Location** (one tap)
4. **Your Name** (optional, can speak)
5. **Submit** (review and send)

**Visual Progress:**
- Big numbered circles (1, 2, 3, 4, 5)
- One step at a time
- Clear instructions
- Can't get lost
